    About ClinConnect

    ClinConnect is a patient follow-up portal developed by Dr. Navavidha Kale as part of her Masterâ€™s Thesis in the Department of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery (OMFS), PGIMER, Chandigarh. The platform is designed to bridge the gap between patients and doctors through a structured, secure, and efficient digital follow-up system. Initially developed for pilot testing, it is fully scalable to accommodate a larger patient population and future integration with a dedicated mobile application.

    Salient Features (Current Version)

    • Secure User Access: Role-based login for doctors and patients using Supabase authentication.
    • Patient Profiles: Each patient has an individual, structured digital profile containing demographic and medical information.
    • Medical History & Treatment Logs: Patients can update and maintain their treatment and recovery details for ongoing follow-ups.
    • Feedback System: Patients can submit follow-up feedback in both text and photo formats.
    • Doctor Dashboard: Doctors can view patient lists, access detailed case histories, and respond to feedback through structured comments.
    • Interactive Timeline: Displays all feedback and doctor comments in reverse chronological order for clear communication flow.
    • Cloud-Integrated Data Storage: Real-time synchronization and secure storage using Supabase for seamless updates without redeployments.
    • Responsive Design: Optimized for use across desktops, tablets, and mobile browsers.

    Future Prospects

    The next phase of ClinConnect envisions integration with a multilingual, AI-enabled, dual-facing virtual agent capable of assisting both patients and clinicians.

    • For Patients: The AI assistant will enable real-time interaction in multiple languages, guiding them through follow-up procedures, medication reminders, and query resolution.
    • For Doctors: It will offer intelligent summarization of patient data, trend-based treatment assessment, and predictive analytics to support clinical decision-making.

    This evolution aims to transform ClinConnect into a smart, inclusive, and accessible digital healthcare companion for the future.
